## How It Helps SEO

### 1. Search Engine Visibility
- **Better indexing**: Structured data helps Google understand your content
- **Rich snippets**: Star ratings, prices show in search results
- **Knowledge graph**: Business info appears in Google's knowledge panel

### 2. Click-Through Rate (CTR)
- **Compelling titles**: Include keywords + benefits
- **Descriptive meta**: Tell users what they'll get
- **Rich results**: Star ratings attract more clicks

### 3. Social Sharing
- **Open Graph**: Beautiful previews on Facebook
- **Twitter Cards**: Eye-catching tweets
- **More shares**: Better social signals = better SEO

### 4. Keyword Targeting
- **Primary keywords**: In title, description, H1
- **Secondary keywords**: Throughout content
- **Long-tail keywords**: In descriptions and content
- **Natural placement**: Not keyword stuffing
